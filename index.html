Bubble Game

  • There is a "Timer" of 1 minute to play this game.
  • And there is "HIT" number, you have to hit the bubbles with same hit number.
  • Once you hit the buble with same as hit number, you will get 10 points every time you hit the correct bubble.
  • But the twist is, when you hit the bubble, every time all the bubbles get changed.
